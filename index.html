March 30, 2026  ·  Lesson 001
Growth Beyond Salvation
Six phases from the new birth through Christlikeness — the call to press on, Spirit-filling, the Word as instrument, restoration of righteousness, the Bema, and conformity to Christ.
March 30, 2026  ·  Lesson 002
The Royal Priest
The invisible commission — your permanent priestly standing before God, direct access to the throne, the offerings the priest brings, and the eternal priesthood that reigns in the Kingdom.
March 30, 2026  ·  Lesson 003
The Royal Ambassador
The outward commission — representing the King of kings to a world that rejected Him. Appointed by God, sustained by His supply, operating by His written Word, until the recall at the Rapture closes the embassy.
March 30, 2026  ·  Lesson 004
Kingdom Reality
The territory purchased by the blood of Christ — already transferred, already inhabited, contested but certain. The Kingdom is not within you, not something you build, and not merely future. It is the present operational reality of every believer.
April 2, 2026  ·  Lesson 005
Faith
The gift, the call, and the response that changes everything. Faith is not a feeling you generate or a quantity you accumulate — it is a gift assigned by God, activated by the Gospel, and expressed in the affirmative response of the will to God's exclusive claim on human destiny.
March 29, 2026  ·  Lesson 000
The God Ordered Life
Man chooses to walk, God plans the steps. An introduction to the curriculum — the three-act shape of a life commanded and arranged by God toward an eternal assignment. Begin here.
April 2, 2026  ·  Lesson 006
Repentance
A change of mind — not a performance of sorrow. μετάνοια stripped of its Latin freight and recovered as the continuous operating principle of the entire Christian life.
April 2, 2026  ·  Lesson 007
Election
An invitation from eternity past — extended to all, received by choice. God has a decree. Jesus controls history. The Spirit guides to all truth. Each individual chooses how closely to follow.
April 2, 2026  ·  Lesson 008
Grace and Love — Part 1
Common grace and the new birth. The rain that falls on the just and unjust. The veil that thins. The Spirit that steps in. Double imputation at the threshold — life and righteousness at once. John 3, Nicodemus.
April 2, 2026  ·  Lesson 009
Grace and Love — Part 2
Efficacious grace and personal love. The detour through Samaria. The living water that becomes a spring inside. One drink becomes rivers. John 4, the Samaritan woman.
April 4, 2026  ·  Lesson 010
Confession
Not a transaction — the approach to the throne. The pipeline is inviolable. Fellowship is the flow. Confession restores it. The first approach was in a garden. The system has never changed.
April 5, 2026  ·  Lesson 011
The Filling of the Holy Spirit
The one revocable ministry. Before Pentecost the Spirit was given and withdrawn. After the cross — permanent indwelling inaugurated. Jesus lived the prototype life in the Spirit operating environment. He cut the path. Then He gave it to us.
